Northeastern University is committed to inventing and demonstrating cutting-edge sustainability solutions, starting with our own campus and community. Husky Ecohub details our efforts on campus, our plans, and climate leadership.

FOCUS AREA

Campus Waste

Practices are focused on reducing, reusing, and recycling a high percentage of materials used in our residence halls, dining areas, classrooms, laboratories, offices, and in construction projects.

Campus Energy

The Energy Management & Commissioning departments actively monitor building energy performance, promptly address anomalies, and conduct upgrades to lighting, heating, cooling, and energy distribution systems to ensure optimal performance and reduced energy loads.

Mutual Learning

Northeastern University has achieved carbon neutrality by acquiring carbon-free, renewable energy credits from offsite wind energy generation facilities.

Climate Action Plan

As we continue to implement new ideas and partner with our community on sustainability and resilience innovations, we will continue to offset our emissions while actively investing in reducing onsite energy use and greenhouse gas emissions.
